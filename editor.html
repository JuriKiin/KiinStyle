Live Design.

You can use this online editor to style your HTML live in the browser! The controls on the left are linked directly to the KiinStyle CSS framework. Once you upload your HTML, you can use them to add the same classes that exist in the framework without all of the coding! Once you are finished with styling your page, you can download the new styled framework. And dont worry! We will automatically link the framework in your HTML so you can go back and edit by yourself!

Whether you're working on a new website, or simply prototyping new components, LiveDesign can help you quickly iterate and design your HTML.

As of right now, you can't directly edit the content of your pages, but that's something we are looking at for the future! But for now, feel free to edit this HTML to your liking, or get started by uploading your own HTML!

We use LiveDesign to do the follwing:

  • Prototype reusable components
  • Design web pages
  • Speed up the development process
